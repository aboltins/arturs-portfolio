About Me

Hi, I'm Arturs, a dedicated and enthusiastic individual pursuing a career as a Software Engineer.

I began my journey in the summer of 2022 by self-studying the fundamentals of front-end web development and having a life-long passion for technology I quickly fell in love with the field.

In the second half of 2022, I enrolled in a full-time EDX Trilogy Skills Bootcamp in Front-End Web Development to further my education and skills. The bootcamp has been an incredibly transformative experience, where I have honed my skills in HTML5, GIT, CSS3, JavaScript (incl. jQuery, ES6, Node.js, npm, Jest and Enzyme). Now before graduation at the end of March 2023 I am ready to tackle the fundamentals of React, as well as the final team project.

The bootcamp has given me numerous opportunities to apply my learning through daily collaborative challenges and weekly individual projects, as well as a recent team of 3 challenge project called "SunTrack". This project allowed me to keep practicing working in an Agile environment and collaborate with my classmates, while utilizing the skills I have learned such as HTML5, Bootstrap, CSS, JavaScript, AJAX API and various JavaScript libraries. This project as well as other most recently finished projects can be seen below in my Work section.

I am ready and very eager to continue learning and applying my skills in a professional setting.
