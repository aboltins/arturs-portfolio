About Me

Hi, I'm Arturs, a dedicated and enthusiastic individual currently interning as a Front End Developer at Happierleads.

I began my journey in 2022 by self-studying the fundamentals of front-end web development and having a life-long passion for technology I quickly fell in love with the field.

In the second half of 2022, I enrolled in a full-time EDX Trilogy Skills Bootcamp in Front-End Web Development to further my education and skills. It was an incredible journey and I am happy to say that at the end of March 2023 I successfully graduated from the bootcamp with a certificate in hand.

Throughout the bootcamp, I was able to refine my skills in HTML5, GIT, CSS3 and JavaScript (including jQuery, ES6, Node.js, npm, Jest, Enzyme and React). The bootcamp provided me with ample opportunities to put my learning into practice through daily collaborative challenges, weekly individual projects and a few larger collaborative projects. Through my work on these assignments, I was able to continue developing my skills in an Agile environment, working closely with others to explore the complexities of Javascript and expand my knowledge of powerful tools such as React and Firebase.

Now, in my role as an intern Front-End Developer, I'm actively applying and expanding my knowledge while working on various aspects of front-end web development. I'm part of a dynamic team, engaging in real-world projects and continuing to learn and grow in this exciting field.
